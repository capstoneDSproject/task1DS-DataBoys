Objective :
To apply knowledge learned in Data Science for real life application.
Goal :
Discovering the importance of washing hands
by reanalyzing the data that made Dr. Ignaz Semmelweis discover it himself.
Problem :
Childbed fever affects every women that had just given birth and as many as 10% women died from it.
Clear Question :
What is it that cause childbed fever and how to prevent it?
How effective is the preventive measure?
Measurable Outcome :
% Mortality rate BEFORE the preventive measure is taken
% Mortality rate AFTER the preventive measure is taken
Data Sources :
The data taken are from two clinics in Vienna General Hospital,Austria in the years between 1841 to 1846
